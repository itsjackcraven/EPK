Jack Craven

Authenticity and passion are the trademarks for Jack Craven’s artistic identity. Known for his complex flows, Craven, born in Toronto, Canada, started writing music at a young age. He spent his early adolescence honing the lyrical, nostalgic style of rap that gained him respect amongst peers & surrounding artists in Southern Ontario.

Blending traditional hip-hop with R&B, dark melodies & hard-hitting trap, Craven has established himself at the forefront of Canada’s hip-hop scene. Jack Craven is also a member of the London-based collective Fresh Religion, a group of artists who produce music, social media content & host live events. This artist is no stranger to the limelight & will continue to be a rising star within the music industry.
